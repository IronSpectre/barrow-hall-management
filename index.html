FAQ's

Your Questions, Answered

Common questions from property owners, leaseholders, and tenants about how we work and what to expect.

What types of properties do you manage?

We manage residential blocks, single lets, HMOs, mixed-use buildings, and commercial properties. Our team is experienced across all property types and sizes.

How do I switch my property to Barrow Hall?

We handle the entire transition process. After an initial consultation, we liaise with your current agent, transfer all documentation, and ensure zero disruption to tenants.

What reporting do owners receive?

Owners receive detailed monthly financial statements, maintenance logs, occupancy updates, and an annual portfolio review. Everything is accessible through our online portal.

How do you handle maintenance emergencies?

We operate a 24/7 emergency helpline. Our vetted contractor network ensures rapid response times, and owners are notified promptly with full cost transparency.

Are you regulated and accredited?

Yes. We are RICS regulated, members of ARMA, and hold Client Money Protection insurance. All funds are held in dedicated, ring-fenced client accounts.

What are your management fees?

Fees are tailored to the scope and complexity of each property. We provide a transparent, all-inclusive quote after an initial property assessment — no hidden charges.
